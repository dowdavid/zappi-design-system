Drawer Content

This is the content area of the drawer. You can put any content here including forms, lists, or any other components.

The drawer slides in from the right with a semi-transparent overlay behind it. Clicking outside the drawer or pressing the Cancel button will close it.

Features:

  • Smooth slide-in animation
  • Semi-transparent backdrop
  • Rounded corners on left side
  • Responsive design
  • Accessible with keyboard support
